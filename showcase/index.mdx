## Share your project

Using Trellis for something cool? We'd like to feature it.

What we're looking for:

- Real projects (not demos)
- Interesting use of specs, tasks, or multi-agent
- Willing to share a brief write-up

What to include:

- Project name and description
- How you use Trellis
- What worked well
- What you'd do differently

Submit via GitHub discussion or email taosu@mindfold.ai.

---

## Showcase criteria

We feature projects that:

**Show real usage** - Not just "installed Trellis", but actually using it for development.

**Teach something** - What can others learn from your setup?

**Are reasonably documented** - We need something to write about.

We don't feature:

- Projects that violate terms of service
- Projects with no public information
- Demos or toy examples (unless they're educational)
